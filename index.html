Simple, effective recipe cataloguing for busy people.

RecipeBuddy makes it easy to keep track of your recipes, ingredients, and implements. Simply add what ingredients you have, and see what you can make!

Update, 4/21/23: RELEASING SOON!

We're proud to announce that RecipeBuddy will be released the weekend of the 22nd, under the MIT open source license. Clone it and use it to your heart's content! RecipeBuddy is completely free to use and modify, so you'll be able to do with it as you'd like. More information will be posted soon as we near the release date.

This website is a work in progress, and will be updated as we continue work on the project. Thank you for bearing with us!
