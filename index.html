Simple recipe cataloguing for busy people.

Whether you're cooking for yourself or the whole family, RecipeBuddy makes it easy to keep track of your recipes, ingredients, and implements. Simply add what ingredients you have, and see what you can make! With support for tagging common allergens, RecipeBuddy makes sure you're always able to make a delicious meal that's right for anyone.
