Try RecipeBuddy online

A sample version of RecipeBuddy is available online. You can use it to explore the app, but you won't be able to save any recipes.

Run RecipeBuddy locally

Get the full functionality of RecipeBuddy in-house. Simply download and run the .jar and navigate to http://localhost:8080 in your browser.
