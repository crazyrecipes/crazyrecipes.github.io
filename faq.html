Frequently Asked Questions

What is RecipeBuddy?

RecipeBuddy is a simple, easy to use recipe & inventory manager that allows you keep track of existing recipes and cooking supplies.

Why was RecipeBuddy created?

RecipeBuddy is our team's project for CS350: Software Design & Development.

What allergens can you filter with RecipeBuddy?

RecipeBuddy currently supports filtering recipes containing peanuts, tree nuts, dairy, soy, gluten, egg, fish, shellfish, corn, sesame, and mustard.
